Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

Momentum is a locally-operated app. All data you enter—workouts, exercises, logs—is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness metrics beyond what you manually enter
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or telemetry

Data Stored on Your Device

The app uses your device's local storage (AsyncStorage) to persist your workout data between sessions. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly back it up using your device's built-in backup features (e.g., iCloud).

Third-Party Services

Momentum does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. As no data is collected at all, the app is safe to use for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the revised version here. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
